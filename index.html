Outcome analysis

Background information:

If we transfect Cas9 protein with sgRNA into the cells, each cell will uptake the RNP complex (Cas9+sgRNA) and this will induce the double strand break, and then the cell will repair that break by NHEJ process. Meaning that each cell will have a different sequence in the target region at the end of the repair process and some of them can repair the same as the original. Therefore, if we amplify the target region, we will get the mixed sequence when Cas9 works well. To identify the mixed sequence population, we will use Sanger sequencing or deep sequencing (NGS).
