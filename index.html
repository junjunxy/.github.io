Getting started

In CRISPR/Cas9 experiments, we will perfrom

1) Guide RNA design 2) Repair prediction 3) Outcome analysis

There are lots of different tools are exist, here we are going to use following web-based toosl to achieve the goal.

1) Guide RNA design - chopchop

2) Repair prediction - FORECast - indelphi

3) Outcome analysis

* Sanger sequencing:  
   - TIDE
   - [TIDE](https://tide.nki.nl/)
   - [ICE analysis](https://ice.synthego.com/#/)
* NGS data: 
   - [CRISPResso2](http://crispresso2.pinellolab.org/submission)
   - [RGENE Tools](http://www.rgenome.net/cas-analyzer/#!)
